Languages

 
 

We use a three-level, Church-centric approach for identifying the fidelity of translated Biblical content:

  1. Level 1: internal — Translator (or team) affirms that translation is in line with Statement of Faith and Translation Guidelines.
  2. Level 2: external — Translation is independently checked and confirmed by at least two others not on the translation team.
  3. Level 3: authenticated — Translation is checked and confirmed by leadership of at least one Church network with native speakers of the language.
